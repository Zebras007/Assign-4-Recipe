Magii Recipe

Ingredients

  • Magii


  • Sauce


  • Salt


  • Water


Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil.


  2. Add magii to boiling water.
    1. Add salt to taste.
    2. Cook for 2 minutes
    3. stirring occasionally.


  3. Add sauce to a pan


  4. Mix cooked magii with sauce.


  5. Enjoy!
